# Georgia Tech: Open Source Opportunities

This page collects URLs for some of the ways that students can engage with open source projects both for pay and for credit. Contributions and updates are welcome via PR.

---

## Paid Opportunities at Georgia Tech

| Program | Compensation | Amount | Who Can Apply | Timing | Key Details | Link |
|---------|-------------|--------|---------------|--------|-------------|------|
| **OSPO Virtual Summer Internship Program (VSIP)** | Paid | Stipend (amount varies) | UG & grad students enrolled at GT with ≥1 semester remaining; no visa restrictions | Summer (mid-May – end of July); ~20 hrs/week, 10 weeks | Contribute to real OSS projects (PyTorch, IBM Analog AI Kit, etc.) with GT & industry mentors; learn Git, code review, OSS workflows | [ospo.cc.gatech.edu/vsip](https://ospo.cc.gatech.edu/vsip/) |
| **PURA Salary Award** *(President's Undergraduate Research Award)* | Paid | $2,000 stipend (competitive) | Undergrads, GPA > 2.0; requires GT/GTRI faculty mentor; one award per academic year | Fall, Spring, or Summer; rolling deadlines | Propose an OSS research project; cannot simultaneously earn course credit for same project; must enroll in free audit course (2698/4698) | [urop.gatech.edu/pura-salary](https://urop.gatech.edu/pura-salary) |
| **PURA Travel Award** | Paid | Travel funding (amount varies) | Undergraduates presenting research at conferences | Rolling basis, any time of year | Funds travel to conferences to present OSS or other research; can be combined with PURA Salary Award in same academic year | [urop.gatech.edu/pura-travel](https://urop.gatech.edu/pura-travel) |
| **GTRI Student Research** *(Georgia Tech Research Institute)* | Paid | Hourly pay (part-time, internship, or co-op) | Primarily EE, CompE, CS majors; UG & grad; 200+ students hired | Year-round; part-time during semesters, full-time co-op/internship options | Applied contract research for federal government and industry; many projects involve OSS tools and software development | [gtri.gatech.edu/careers](https://www.gtri.gatech.edu/careers/students) |

## Hybrid (Paid + Credit) Programs

These programs may provide either pay or credit depending on the project and the advisor.

| Program | Compensation | Amount / Credit | Who Can Apply | Timing | Key Details | Link |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|---------------|--------|-------------|------|
| **UROP** *(Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program)* | Paid + Credit | Connects to PURA (paid) or course credit options | All GT undergraduates, any major | Year-round; seminars, symposium, newsletters | Campus-wide umbrella program; connects students to both paid (PURA Salary) and for-credit research; hosts annual symposium and "Jumpstart"/"Accelerate" workshops | [urop.gatech.edu](https://urop.gatech.edu/) |
| **UROP Spring Symposium** | Recognition | No direct pay | All GT undergraduates doing research | Annual (spring semester) | Campus-wide showcase for undergraduate research; students can present OSS projects as talks or posters | [symposium.urop.gatech.edu/](https://symposium.urop.gatech.edu/) |
| **UROC Research Jobs** *(Undergraduate Research Opportunities in Computing)* | Paid + Credit | Varies by lab/faculty | Computing undergraduates at GT | Annual job fair (typically fall); positions ongoing | Faculty present open research positions at annual job fair; browse paid openings in the research database; heavily involves OSS tools | [cc.gatech.edu/uroc](https://www.cc.gatech.edu/undergraduate-research-opportunities-computing-uroc) |

## For-Credit Opportunities

| Program | Compensation | Credit | Who Can Apply | Timing | Key Details | Link |
|---------|-------------|--------|---------------|--------|-------------|------|
| **VIP Program** *(Vertically Integrated Projects)* | For Credit | 1–2 credits/semester; up to 6 total | All GT undergraduates (sophomores and up) as well as graduate students; multidisciplinary; up to 3 years | Fall & Spring semesters; long-term multi-semester teams | Join a research team with faculty + grad students; many VIP teams build or use open source software; vertically integrated (soph through PhD on same team) | [vip.gatech.edu](https://vip.gatech.edu/) |
| **Research Option (RO)** | For Credit | Academic credit + transcript designation | All GT undergraduates; especially suited for future grad school applicants | Typically junior/senior year; spans 2+ semesters | In-depth research culminating in publication or conference presentation; transcript notation; can focus on OSS development with faculty advisor | [undergradresearch.gatech.edu/research-option](https://undergradresearch.gatech.edu/research-option)<br />[coe.gatech.edu/undergrad-research](https://coe.gatech.edu/research/undergraduate-student-research) |
| **Independent Research Courses** *(Course numbers 2699 / 4699)* | For Credit | Academic credit (letter grade) | All GT undergraduates; requires faculty agreement | Any semester; student arranges directly with faculty | Work directly with a faculty member on any research project, including OSS; also counts toward Honors Program (JMHP) curriculum | [undergradresearch.gatech.edu](https://undergradresearch.gatech.edu/opportunities-georgia-tech) |
| **OMSCS Independent Research** *(CS 8903)* | For Credit | Academic credit (graduate level) | OMSCS students; requires faculty approval | Any semester; faculty email advertised opportunities before each semester | Faculty-advertised research projects each semester via email and Ed Discussion; students apply and sign up for independent study credit (CS 8903); can also do thesis/project-based master's (CS 6999) | [omscs.gatech.edu/research-opportunities](https://omscs.gatech.edu/research-opportunities) |
| **OMSCS Research Assistantships** | Paid (rare) | Varies | OMSCS students; same eligibility as on-campus master's students | Ongoing; check individual lab websites | While uncommon, OMSCS students can apply for the same RA positions as on-campus master's students; check individual lab and research center websites directly | [omscs.gatech.edu/research-opportunities](https://omscs.gatech.edu/research-opportunities) |
| **OMSCS Conference Funding** *(Research Publication Support)* | Paid (reimbursement) | Conference registration or journal publication fees covered | OMSCS students and alumni whose research is accepted to a reputable conference or peer-reviewed journal | Rolling; began Fall 2025 | Research accepted to a reputable venue can have registration or publication fees covered; encourages OMSCS students to publish and present OSS or CS research | [omscs.gatech.edu/research-opportunities](https://omscs.gatech.edu/research-opportunities) |

## Community & External Pathways

| Program | Compensation | Amount | Who Can Apply | Timing | Key Details | Link |
|---------|-------------|--------|---------------|--------|-------------|------|
| **LibreTech Collective** *(GT's FLOSS student club)* | Volunteer / Community | No pay; builds skills & portfolio | Any GT student | Year-round; ongoing | GT's only Free/Libre/Open-Source software club; all projects are GPL-compatible and led by GT students, faculty, and alumni; great entry point before applying to VSIP or PURA | [librelinker.us](https://librelinker.us/) |
| **Google Summer of Code (GSoC)** | External Paid | ~$1,500–$6,600 depending on project size & location | Any enrolled GT student (18+); apply independently to open source orgs | Summer; applications typically open January–March | Work on real OSS projects for 8–22 weeks with external mentors; GT OSPO can help connect students to relevant org projects | [summerofcode.withgoogle.com](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) |
